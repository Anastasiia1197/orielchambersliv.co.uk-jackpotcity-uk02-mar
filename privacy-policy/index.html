JackpotCity UK Privacy Policy – Your Data, Clearly Protected

Last updated: 15 March 2026

At JackpotCity UK, we place the utmost importance on the protection of your personal data. This privacy policy is designed to explain clearly how we collect, use, share and protect your information when you use our website at https://orielchambersliv.co.uk/. We are fully committed to complying with the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR), the Data Protection Act 2018, and all other applicable British data protection legislation.

What Data We Collect and How It Enhances Your Experience

In order to provide you with the best possible service as a UK player, we may collect the following types of data:

  • Voluntarily provided personal information: When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, phone number, date of birth, and other information required to deliver our services and comply with UKGC verification requirements.
  • Browsing data: We gather information about your interaction with our site, including your IP address, browser type, pages visited, and the duration of your sessions.
  • Location data: With your consent, we may collect information about your location to confirm eligibility for UK-regulated gaming services and to personalise the experience.
  • Payment information: Financial transactions are processed by fully accredited and secure payment providers; we do not store your card or banking details on our servers.

This data allows us to:

  1. Personalise your experience on the JackpotCity UK platform according to your preferences

  2. Continuously improve our services, features, and game recommendations for British players

  3. Provide you with efficient and personalised customer support available around the clock

  4. Ensure the security of your account and prevent fraud or unauthorised access

  5. Keep you informed of relevant news and promotional offers where you have explicitly given your consent

The Essential Role of Cookies in Personalising Your JackpotCity Experience

Cookies are small text files stored on your device that improve your experience on our site. We use different types of cookies for specific purposes:

Cookie Type Purpose Retention Period
Essential cookies Core site functionality (login, session management, security) Session or 12 months
Performance cookies Site usage analysis and continuous improvement 24 months
Functionality cookies Remembering your preferences and settings 12 months
Targeting cookies Personalised promotions and content (with consent) 12 months

You can manage cookies through your browser settings at any time. Please note that disabling certain cookies may limit some features and functionality available on our site. For full details, please refer to our Cookie Policy.

How We Use Your Data Responsibly to Improve Our Services

We use your data in strict accordance with the purposes for which it was collected and the legal bases applicable under UK data protection law:

  • Contract performance: To provide the services you have requested, manage your player account, and process transactions in GBP (£).
  • Legitimate interests: To improve our services, prevent fraud, ensure platform security, and fulfil our obligations as a UKGC-licensed operator.
  • Consent: To send you marketing communications where you have explicitly agreed to receive them, in line with UK PECR regulations.
  • Legal obligation: To comply with applicable UK laws, UKGC licence conditions, anti-money laundering regulations, and any relevant court orders.

We only process your data for as long as is necessary to fulfil the purposes for which it was collected, in accordance with applicable UK legal requirements and our regulatory obligations.

Strict Protocols for Sharing Data with Trusted Partners

We do not sell or rent your personal data to any third parties. We may share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

  1. Service providers: Trusted partners who assist us in operating our site, including hosting, analytics, and payment processing providers, all of whom are bound by strict confidentiality agreements and UK GDPR compliance obligations.

  2. Legal authorities: Where required by UK law, by a court order, or in response to a lawful request from a regulatory authority such as the UK Gambling Commission or the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO).

  3. Business partners: Only with your prior explicit consent and in compliance with all applicable UK data protection legislation.

All our partners are carefully selected and contractually required to protect your data in accordance with the UK GDPR. In the event of any data transfer outside the UK, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place as required by law.

Your Rights to Access, Amend or Delete Your Data at Any Time

Under the UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018, you have the following rights in relation to your personal data:

  • Right of access: Obtain confirmation that we are processing your data and receive a copy of it.
  • Right to rectification: Request the correction of inaccurate or incomplete data held about you.
  • Right to erasure: Request the deletion of your personal data in certain circumstances.
  • Right to restriction: Request that we limit our processing of your data in specific situations.
  • Right to object: Object to the processing of your data on legitimate grounds.
  • Right to data portability: Receive your data in a structured, commonly used, machine-readable format.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact our Data Protection Officer (DPO) at privacy@orielchambersliv.co.uk. We will respond within one calendar month in accordance with UK data protection law.

Keeping You Informed – Timely Updates to Our Privacy Policy

We are committed to informing you of any material changes to this privacy policy. Updates will be published on this page with the date of the most recent revision clearly indicated. For significant changes, we will notify you by email or via a prominent notice on the JackpotCity UK website.

We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information. Your continued use of our services following any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
